Self-introduction

Learn several sentences to describe who you are.

Yes, let's do it!

Wait...Why that?

Brief Explanation

  1. Talking about yourself is the most relevant content to know.
  2. You'll be learning a lot of vocabulary, structure, and grammar as well.
  3. Speak up to one minute in your target language, all by yourself.

Now I get it. Let's start!
