Greetings

Learn several formal/informal expressions to greet and introduce yourself.

Start

Why?

Why?

  1. There are many variations of greetings. Get to know most of them!
  2. Begin to personalize your new language: focus on what pleases your ears.
  3. Learn day-to-day expressions and you will not sound formal to a speaker.
