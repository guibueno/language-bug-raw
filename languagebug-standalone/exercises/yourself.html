Yourself

Learn several sentences to describe who you are.

Start

Why?

Why?

  1. Talking about yourself is the most relevant content to know.
  2. It covers a lot of vocabulary, sentence structures, and even grammar.
  3. You'll be able to speak for ~1min in your target language by yourself.
